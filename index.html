About Us

Anchor of Hope Church is a place where hearts are lifted in worship and lives are transformed through the power of God’s Word. Located in Yenagoa, Bayelsa State, we are dedicated to glorifying God and making disciples of all nations.

Address: Etegwe, along Isaac Boro Expressway, Yenagoa, Bayelsa State, Nigeria
